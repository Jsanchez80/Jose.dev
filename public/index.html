ABOUT

Growth and development are high on my list of values. And much like people, businesses must continue to grow and develop or face extinction from their competitors. During my career I sought tools that would allow my teams and businesses to operate efficiently, while increasing productivity and empowering individuals to take initiative.

Often times technology was available to aid in our success, however the functionality and inaccessability of these tools rendered them incapable of performing to standard or insufficient in their operating parameters. I found myself developing a tool-box of pre-formatted materiel that functioned as needed but it meant having an on-hand system of huge index files full of paper work or binders/SOP manuals.

Over the years, tehnology would allow me to create forms and layouts utilizing the MS Office Suites, etc, yet I felt there was still a void in accomplishing organizational goals. While laptops had come of age, transfer of knowledge and user experience kept us from monitoring success parameters.

As I developed in my career I would begin researching problems, planning, developing and the implementing these processes to other locations or cities across the U.S.; Because of this, I took note of industry practices and made the decision to pursue a career in software development. My experience has afforded me an opportunity to help like minded business men and women accomplish their organizational goals through executing flawless systems designed to impact recruitment, retention, and development while increasing sales and productivity and fostering a heightened state of morale.
