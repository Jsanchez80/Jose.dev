ABOUT

Our History:

Although I enjoyed working with a stellar team iIn my last position, as a Carpenter for Leonard Contracting, I felt something was missing. I didn’t feel fulfilled or satisfied with my day to day tasks. My leadership skills were underutilized and I felt as though I had little opportunity for future growth. I knew something had to change and started reflecting on my professional history to decide where i wanted to go next.

My Development

During each phase of my career, I've always searched for the proper tools in order to streamline productivity and improve performance, while empowering my teams to make the best decisions possible in a timely manner. And although technology had been accessible to me as an aid in the process of pre-shift/preparation, forecasting and data collection, I felt the existing tools hadn’t evolved much from virtual dashboards and fell short in providing data necessary to remain productive in my field. Because of this, I started thinking about pursuing a career in technology. I remembered CodeuUp, a program for software developers that my sister had mentioned to me. And ultimately, I decided to take the leap and enroll.

Our Future:

I entered Codeup in July of 2016, with no experience in software programming, but have brought a wealth of knowledge from my leadership roles and working elbow to elbow with my team mates.

I believe through execution of flawless systems designed to impact recruitment, retention, and development/transfer of knowledge, we can better understand our businesses and provide solutions catered toward impacting your organization.

I’m not sure exactly what the future holds for me, but I look forward to combining my problem solving skills, unique talents and leadership skills with my new coding skills to add value and integrity to my prospective employer. I welcome you to leave your feedback and if you'd love to know more about me or EVOQ, contact me below. Thank you and I look forward to working with you. "You have a dream, lets EVOQ IT!"
